Introduction To Technopreneurship

What is Technopreneurship?

As it is hinted by the term, it is a blend of two words such as “technology” and “Entrepreneurship”. Thoroughly, it is a kind of entrepreneurship in the field of technology. The process of technopreneurship is a combination of technological advancements and entrepreneurial skills. In the products and services transformation, an integral part of technopreneur is technology.

Importance of Technopreneurship

Technopreneurship plays an important role in between technology and the day to day lives of every individual. One very evident application of this concept is it makes easy for people to stay in touch with each other and comes up with some unpredictable products as well as solutions that are beneficial for the nation. Here are some examples of the importance of technopreneurship:


• Creating Employment Opportunities
• Local Resources
• Business diversification and decentralization
• Technological advancement
• Capital formation
• Promotion of entrepreneurial activities



You want to become a successful Technopreneur?

The world and the path of technopreneurship is not as easy as you think. Maybe some would consider it is as easy as just develop an application/software then its done, but it's not, it takes a lot of hardwork and patience for you to become successful in this field. Here are some steps on how to become a Technopreneur:


• Build a team
• Boosts problem-solving skills
• The decision on the final strategy
